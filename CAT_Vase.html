CAT Vase

As the name suggests, this is a plant vase for my cat (Charlie). When his cat tree was slowly wearing off, i got the idea to design something new for him that might peak his interest. Charlie loves to chew on cat grass, so i thought "might as well design something cute to put it in". I simply worked with cartboard, hemp rope and some glue. The long part itself can be scratched on, whereas the top part provides easy access to catfriendly plants.
