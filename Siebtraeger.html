Siebtraeger READY MADE

Here, "Ready-made" has a twist. Our professor asked us to make a design out of an everyday-item, so i chose a mini strainer. After cutting holes, attaching silver rings to the metal and baking a base out of clay, i design an object that could be used as an accessory stand. In this case i hung some earrings and smaller items onto the metal part of the stand. I really liked the shiny and dainty look of it.
